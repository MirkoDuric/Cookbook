Kabob Koobideh (Persian Grilled Kebabs) Ingredients

  • 500 g samp(cracked corn) and beans
  • 30 ml oil
  • 2 tsp garlic flakes
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 1 green pepper, deseeded and diced
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tbsp Mild Masala curry powder
  • 1 tbsp mixed herbs
  • 500 g beef stewing steak
  • 1 x 400 g tin chopped tomatoes
  • 500 ml beef stock
  • salt and black pepper to taste
