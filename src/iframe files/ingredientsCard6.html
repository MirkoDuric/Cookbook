Ćevapi Ingredients

    For the Ćevapi
  • 600 g finely minced beef (1.3 pounds)
  • 300g finely minced lamb (or pork mince if you prefer) (10 oz)
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon fine salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
    • Serve With:
  • Turkish-style Flatbread
  • Chopped raw onions
  • Ajvar
