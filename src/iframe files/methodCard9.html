Qormesabzi Preparation Method

  1. Heat 2 tablespoons oil in a large pot over medium-high heat
  2. Add onion
    • cook and stir until deep golden brown, 10 to 15 minutes
  3. Stir in turmeric for 1 to 2 minutes
  4. Add chuck cubes; cook until coated in turmeric and browned on all sides, 8 to 10 minutes
  5. Heat remaining 2 tablespoons oil in a separate pot over medium heat
  6. Add spinach, green onions, parsley, cilantro, chives, and fenugreek leaves
    • cook and stir until deep dark green in color, 5 to 10 minutes
  7. Stir spinach mixture into onion and chuck mixture
  8. Pour in enough water to create a slurry consistency
  9. Season with salt and pepper
  10. Stir in lemon juice
  11. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer stew until greens soften, about 1 hour
  12. Pierce dried limes with a fork; add to stew
  13. Continue simmering until chuck is tender, 30 minutes to 1 hour
  14. Stir in red kidney beans
  15. Cook until flavors combine, about 30 more minutes
    16. Discard dried limes before serving
